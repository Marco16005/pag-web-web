Our Game

'Campus Chaos' plunges players into the heart of Tecnológico de Monterrey, now overrun by unexpected threats. As a student, you'll navigate familiar yet transformed locations, from lecture halls taken over by hostile entities to libraries hiding secret passages. The game combines elements of action, adventure, and puzzle-solving. Players can choose from unique characters like Tomas, Emma, and Teus, each with special abilities. Complete missions, gather resources, upgrade your gear, and team up with other students (or go solo) to uncover the source of the chaos and reclaim your campus. Expect a blend of humor, suspense, and challenging gameplay that will keep you on your toes!
